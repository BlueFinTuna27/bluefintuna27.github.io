  • Tech Public Policy

    Florida Medicaid & Disability Benefits Guide

    Florida's disability benefits system runs through three agencies that rarely coordinate — and the hardest gate, a federal disability redetermination at age 18, pushes more than half of childhood SSI recipients off their benefits nationally, even when nothing about their condition has changed. Built a bilingual (EN/ES) tool that reads an agency's actual DCF, APD, or SSA notice, explains it in plain language, and walks a caregiver through the hardest step: SSA's Adult Function Report, the form that decides most cases. Built during an internship at Jupiter Gardens Primary Care.

    55.7% initially denied at 18 3 agencies reconciled 2 languages from day one

    Care Coordination Assistant

    Built for a small independent primary care practice whose older patients often see three or four specialists at once. Reads a visit transcript, flags the referrals and specialist updates a physician mentioned, drafts the outbound message, and tracks it through to sent — nothing leaves the office without a person approving it first.     Housing Affordability & Economic Mobility

    Built a two-phase econometric model analyzing 60+ housing-market drivers across 63 public datasets. Phase one: multivariate regression identifying rent, housing supply, and income as the leading unaffordability predictors. Phase two: XGBoost/ARIMA time-series forecasting projecting a 6% rise in unaffordability by 2030.
